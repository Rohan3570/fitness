About us

Fitness Striker is a comprehensive and specialized physical education program developed for the early childhood age and A much needed approach to Fitness through physical education and sports . Fitness striker PE program for children covering Kindergarten, Primary and High School. Each module is designed to develop age-specific physical attributes and learning objectives.

Fitness Striker believes in making sports an integral part of the education of a child and is working towards developing A healthier and fitter generation by using a structured sports and physical Education curriculum and to motivate a generation of kids and adults to be physically fit by giving them an opportunity to learn and play.

Fitness Striker introducing a Age appropriate sports and fitness school curriculum at an early stage so that is can serve as a building block for the generations.

We at Fitness striker develop physical agility of kids through a research based curriculum which is well amalgamated with the regular school syllabus to give them a better cumulative learning. Our unique model also trains children in a rewarding way to be successful in their studies as well by integrating multiple skills.

Internationally Researched Curriculum: The program lesson plan focus on developing Fundamental Movement Skills (loco motor, objects control and stability skills), rudimentary movements, Reflexes and reactions as per the standards of National association of sports and physical education (U.S.A).

The Program will cater to the students from Grade Play onwards. We will provide standards-based activities, skill cards, task cards and templates required for effective deployment of the curriculum.

The lesson plans will be implemented for the entire academic year in a structured and phased manner. The same will be based on the school’s time-table with a judicious mix of fitness and sports skills. The curriculum will be integrated into the school's current PE program and will be deployed during school hours.
