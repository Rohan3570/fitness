About us

Fitness Striker is a comprehensive and specialized physical education program developed for the early childhood age and A much needed approach to Fitness through physical education and sports . Fitness striker PE program for children covering Kindergarten, Primary and High School. Each module is designed to develop age-specific physical attributes and learning objectives.

Fitness Striker believes in making sports an integral part of the education of a child and is working towards developing A healthier and fitter generation by using a structured sports and physical Education curriculum and to motivate a generation of kids and adults to be physically fit by giving them an opportunity to learn and play.

The Program will cater to the students from Grade Play onwards. We will provide standards-based activities, skill cards, task cards and templates required for effective deployment of the curriculum.
